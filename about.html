About Waste to Fertilizer

Turning compost into community 🌍

Waste to Fertilizer is a social sustainability initiative rooted in the idea that organic waste isn’t garbage—it’s potential. We convert biodegradable waste into nutrient-rich compost for gardens, farms, and homes, working with local partners to make eco-living easy and accessible. Our program reduces methane emissions, promotes food security, and teaches composting through workshops and resources. Whether you're a household, restaurant, school, or urban grower, we empower you to take part in the green cycle. Join our compost community and grow something that matters—because every peel and petal counts 🌱
